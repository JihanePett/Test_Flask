My name is Catherine, With hindsight, I have noticed that with another culture, another country, other people, gastronomy is my point of reference or comparison. Through food, I can feel a sense of home and sense of community. Driven by a culinary curiosity, an interest in new tastes and original shapes my favourite go to place are the local markets and places, where i can discover special spices, local vegetables and fruits. Whenever I have the opportunity, I sneak in local kitchens to furtively check on and learn cooking techniques. You understand, I love people, travels and food. Like many of us!
